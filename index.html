About Me

I am an experienced Music Producer and Sound Designer with a solid background in Game Audio.

Based in the Netherlands, I specialize in creating adaptive music, interactive sound design, and immersive audio experiences for video games. With over a decade of experience in the industry, I've had the privilege of working on critically acclaimed titles and collaborating with talented studios worldwide.

My approach combines technical expertise with creative artistry—from composing emotionally resonant soundtracks to designing intricate audio systems that respond dynamically to gameplay.
